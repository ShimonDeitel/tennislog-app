Privacy Policy

Tennislog is an offline-first app. All entries you log are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, and no third-party trackers in this app.

Purchases (if any) are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. We have no server, so we have nothing to give out even if asked.
